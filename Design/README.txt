Hello, welcome to our design!

In the BusinessRules folder, you'll find all the business rule specs that we have defined

FieldSpecifications holds ALL of the used field specifications in our current design

The Relationships folder holds our Table Relationships matrix and list, as well as .PNGs of
the relationship diagrams/linking tables

The ViewSpecs folder holds all of the view specifications created for this design

Outside of these folders are some interesting files. 
Deliverable5_ERDiagram-ER Diagram.png is the PNG file depicting our full ER diagram
mission.txt holds our mission statement/objectives
tables_and_fields.txt holds a list of all of our base tables and their related fields
UI_Flow_Diagram.jpg holds the full UI flow for our design

Hope you find everything you need!
